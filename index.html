Welcome to the Scottish Norwegian Society

The Scottish Norwegian Society in Glasgow brings together people with an interest in Norway, Norwegian culture, history, language, and traditions. We meet regularly for talks, cultural evenings, celebrations, and social events.

Our programme runs from September to June, and includes:

  • Lectures and cultural presentations
  • Musical evenings and social gatherings
  • Annual Julebasar (Christmas Fair)
  • Special events celebrating Norwegian traditions

New members are always welcome.
