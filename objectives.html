Objectives

The Scottish Norwegian Society exists to promote friendship, cultural understanding, and shared traditions between Scotland and Norway.

Our objectives include:

  • Encouraging interest in Norwegian culture, history, and language.
  • Organising talks, events, and celebrations throughout the year.
  • Supporting connections between Norwegians living in Scotland and the wider community.
  • Preserving and sharing the heritage of both nations.
